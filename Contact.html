Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical website project take?

The timeline depends on the project complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects with custom features may take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we offer various support packages to ensure your website continues to run smoothly. This includes security updates, performance optimization, and content updates.

What information do you need to start a project?

We'll need details about your business, target audience, design preferences, and specific functionality requirements. The more information you can provide, the better we can tailor the solution to your needs.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients from all over the world. Our remote collaboration process is streamlined to ensure smooth communication regardless of location.
